Bučení returns for its ninth edition on 31 July - 1 August 2026, at Přespolní in Lubná u Poličky, Czech Republic - two days of experimental electronic music and audio-visual art.

You can get to the festival by train or bus. Train connections go to the stations Česká Třebová or Polička. From there you have to take a bus to Lubná. The bus stop in Lubná is called Obecní úřad (the municipality office), and from there you can walk to Přespolní and the festival Bučení.

Train and bus connections can be found at this link.

Visitors have the option to camp or sleep outside under the stars (bring your own gear). There will be a kitchen with plant-based options and a bar.

Bučení is organised by the Přespolní association based in the Czech countryside. Since 2017, Přespolní has been organising exhibitions, residencies, Bučení festival, and cultural and educational programmes. The festival is supported by the Czech Ministry of Culture, the State Fund of Culture, the Pardubice Region, the Visegrad Fund, and the municipality of Lubná.
